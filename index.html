Wayfinding meets awareness

Three things Nsoromma does brilliantly

  • Turn-by-turn voice guidance

    Say where you want to go and Nsoromma speaks each step of the route — distances, turns and landmarks — guiding you door to door without ever needing to look at the screen.

  • Real-time hazard alerts

    The camera watches the path ahead and warns you about moving vehicles, pedestrians and obstacles the white cane misses — before you reach them, not after.

  • Hands-free, audio-first

    Everything works through speech and sound, so your hands stay free for your cane or surroundings. Ask questions on the move and Nsoromma answers aloud.

Why Nsoromma

Built for the way Ghana actually moves.

Nsoromma means “child of the heavens” — the Adinkra star that speaks of guidance and hope. We built it because the existing aids fall short: the white cane finds only what's within arm's reach, guide dogs are scarce and costly, and apps like Google Maps assume a sighted user watching a screen — blind to the vehicles, pedestrians and obstacles right in front of you.

Nsoromma is the first to combine wayfinding with real-time situational awareness in a hands-free, audio-first experience. It was built and tested on the busy University of Ghana, Legon campus — its roads, footpaths and crowds — and is designed to travel beyond it to streets, markets and public spaces. Independence isn't a feature we added; it's the reason the app exists.

Quick & safe to set up

How to install Nsoromma on Android

Nsoromma is shared as an APK file you install directly. Follow these five steps in order. Each step is short, and you can do it with a screen reader running.

  1. 1

    Download the APK

    Select the Download APK button and wait for the file nsoromma.apk to finish downloading.

  2. 2

    Allow this install

    When your phone asks, choose Settings, then turn on “Allow from this source.” This lets your browser install the app.

  3. 3

    Open the file

    Go back and select the downloaded file. Choose Install and wait a few seconds for it to finish.

  4. 4

    Grant permissions

    Open Nsoromma. Allow Location, Camera and Microphone access so navigation, hazard alerts and voice can work.

  5. 5

    Start walking

    Press “Tap to speak” and say where you'd like to go. That's it — Nsoromma guides you from here.
